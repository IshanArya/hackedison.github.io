I WANT TO HACK I WANT TO MENTOR



About

HackEHS will be a 24 hour hackathon at Edison High School, open to students across the country.The event will host over 400 attendees, including participants, volunteers, mentors, judges, and sponsors. Anyone can come - you don't need to know how to code! There'll be plenty of services available to you - food, workshops, hackable hardware, and a bunch of other cool things -- all free!




Questions?



Do I need previous experience?

Absolutely not! Hackathons are for beginners as well as seasoned developers. If you don't have experience with programming, we'll have plenty of workshops where you'll be able to pick up the basics.

How do I get there?

HackEHS will be located at Edison High School, Edison, New Jersey. If you'd like a map, then click here. We'll have staff outside the building that'll direct you to the main event, as well as give you a map of the school in case you get lost.

Who is allowed to attend?

All highschoolers are allowed to attend, from the U.S and other countries as well. If you feel you're too old or young to attend, but have experience, you can contact us.




What if I get hungry?

Fear not! We'll have a plethora of food choices for our guests. We encourage attendees to drop by the main lobby where all the refreshments will be served (even at midnight!). Vegetarian options will be available too.

What should I bring?

Be sure to bring a laptop and a cellphone with chargers, as these 2 devices are what you'll mainly be using during the event. They're pretty important if you'll want to hack. Sleeping bags and power strips will be convenient as well.

How much will it cost?

All attendees must pay a fee of $5000 It's free, of course! Hackathons are supposed to be a learning experience, and we don't think there should be a price on education.
